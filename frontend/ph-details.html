    Why pH Matters

    pH tells us whether water is acidic or alkaline. Safe drinking water should be between 6.5 and 8.5. Outside this range, water can corrode pipes, harm aquatic life, and even affect human health. Monitoring pH ensures water is not only safe to drink but also safe for cooking, cleaning, and everyday use.
